Hi, ich mag Bilder, Strukturen, und Strukturen in Bildern. Also habe ich diese Seite erstellt, um sie zu sammeln.

Hi, I like pictures, structures, and structures in pictures. So I made this page to collect them.

The images you can see on this site are created from satellite imagery. For this, I used data from the European Satellites Sentinel 1 and 2. The data is freely available, when attributed correctly (thus the footer). I publish my work (the pictures and descriptions) here under the license CC BY-SA, which means you are free to share and adapt, as long you cite me and this site as the author (Jonathan Bahlmann, jonathom.github.io) and publish your work under CC BY-SA as well. If you want any image in a higher resolution, feel free to contact me. As this is a work in progress, I can't guarantee that the stuff I say in the descriptions is absolutely correct.

You can find this project on github.

Contains modified Copernicus imagery. Jonathan Bahlmann 2021.
