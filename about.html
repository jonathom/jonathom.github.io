About this Page

Hi, I like images, structures, and imaged structures. So I made this page to collect them.

The images you can see on this site are created from satellite imagery. I mostly used data from the European Copernicus Programme, namely from the satellites Sentinel 1 and 2. The data is freely available, when attributed correctly (thus the footer). I publish my work (the images) here under the license CC BY-NC 4.0, which means you are free to share and adapt, as long you cite me and this site as the author (Jonathan Bahlmann, https://jonathom.github.io) and don't make use of my work or your adaption commercially. If you think about (re)using my work or have already done it, I would love to hear about it! Also, if you would like to have any image in a higher resolution, feel free to contact me.

You can find this website on github.

Feel free to contact me at jonathanbahlmann [@] web.de
